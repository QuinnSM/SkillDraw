Lesson 3 - Exercise A

In these exercises, you will be drawing a self portrait. Before you begin, either take a selfie or find a mirror (mirror is suggested). Once you have a clear reference of your face, begin drawing it with sufficient and accurate details. Make sure to begin from the outside, create an outline, and then work your way into the middle of your face. Only use grey and black tones and focus on outline structure.
