Lesson 1: Line Fundamentals

Simplify complex objects with line construction to buff up your drawing skillz

Considering complex figures in fundamental lines and shapes will help you understand how to build those complex objects from scratch. First, you must begin with all initial detail and slowly simplify the image until you have identified the bare requirements to communicate your larger shape. Once you have mastered this, you can reverse it and create still life quality drawings without even looking at the object. In this lesson we will use different types of lines to accomplish this form of simplification.
