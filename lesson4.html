Lesson 4: Landscaping

Use perspective, form, and depth to redesign the world around you.

This lesson is about balance: between reality and the imagination; between the beautiful chaos of nature and the rigid structure of civilization. An experienced artist should possess the ability to accurately depict a real landscape with correct perspective and proportion while still being able to imagine and translate their own fictional worlds onto the canvas. In this series of exercises, you will be able to draw natural scenery and then impose your own urban environment on top of it. You will embark on a journey through space and time with nothing but your mind and pen leading the way.
