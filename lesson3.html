Lesson 3: Facial Deconstruction

Identifying abstract shapes and buidling blocks in the human face

For many artists, faces are one of the most challenging objects to draw accurately. In order to aid you in this endeavor, these exercises will provide various lenses to view a face through. By deconstructing a face into individual elements (outline, interior shading, and geometric shapes), you will find drawing a face from scratch to be much easier. As the last lesson on abstracting complex shapes, you must learn to identify all the building blocks within the face, a skill that will benefit you greatly in your drawing career.
